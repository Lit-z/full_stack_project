Gallery


Images based on how exoplanets can potentially look based on the information we know so far



55 Cancri e

Concept of a hot, rocky exoplanet called 55 Cancri e, which is nearly two times as wide as Earth. It has a drastic temperature difference between day and night sides that suggests lava flows are significant

Kepler 62e

The artist's concept depicts Kepler-62e, a super-Earth-size planet in the habitable zone of a star smaller and cooler than the sun, located about 1,200 light-years from Earth in the constellation Lyra.

Kepler 69c

Concept of Kepler-69c which is a super-Earth about 70% bigger than Earth with an orbit that resembles Venus

Planet orbiting a white dwarf, PG 0010+280

This artist's concept shows a hypothetical "rejuvenated" planet based on tentative evidence, a gas giant that has reclaimed its youthful infrared glow.

Kepler 22b

Kepler 22b orbits its star in the habitable zone and the planet is 2.4 times the size of Earth
